Our Story

Built by Family.
Built for Indiana.

Lopez Painting was founded in 2003 by the Lopez family right here in Indiana. What started as a small painting operation has grown into a trusted name across the state — built entirely on word of mouth, hard work, and a commitment to doing the job right every single time.

We are family owned and family operated — meaning when you call us, you're talking to the people who will actually show up and do the work. No middlemen, no subcontractors. Just the Lopez family, showing up for yours.

From a single room repaint to a full exterior transformation, we treat every project like it's our own home. That's not just a saying — it's how we've operated for over 20 years.
